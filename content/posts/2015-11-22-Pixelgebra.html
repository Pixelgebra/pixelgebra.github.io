Pixel + algebra = Pixelgebra

22 Nov 2015 BY YYC . misc

We make game. We play with functional programming. We are Pixelgebra.

Nowadays functional language is already quite practical. It has always been some rumors about how fast functional developments could be. Is that true? if so, can that bring us any advantage for game developing? Pixelgebra aims to find out an answer for that question!

Pixelgebra is (so far) a team of two. We play with Haskell. We love games. So, can we put everything we love together? Can we show that functional programming is useful for game development? Could functional programming be a good choice of producing commercial games? To find out the possibility, Pixelgebra is trying to use functional language to achieve some techniques of rapidly game developing.
